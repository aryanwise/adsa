## 🎯 Immediate Analytical Goals  

1. **Revenue & Volume Trend Analysis**  
   - **Objective**: Quantify month‑over‑month changes in total revenue and total items sold, segmented by `CUSTOMER_SEGMENT`.  
   - **Why**: Reveals seasonality, growth patterns, and segment‑specific performance.  
   - **Approach**: Group by `MONTH_KEY` × `CUSTOMER_SEGMENT`, aggregate `SUM(TOTAL_REVENUE_USD)` and `SUM(ITEM_COUNT)`, then plot time series or waterfall charts.

2. **Customer‑Segment Profitability Profiling**  
   - **Objective**: Compute average revenue per item (`TOTAL_REVENUE_USD / ITEM_COUNT`) and average order value per segment.  
   - **Why**: Identifies which segments generate the highest margin per unit and where upsell opportunities exist.  
   - **Approach**: Create derived metric `REVENUE_PER_ITEM`, then compare distributions across the four segments using box‑plots or statistical tests (ANOVA/Kruskal‑Wallis).

3. **Predictive Order‑Size Modeling**  
   - **Objective**: Build a lightweight regression model (e.g., linear regression, Random Forest) to predict `TOTAL_REVENUE_USD` from `MONTH_KEY`, `CUSTOMER_SEGMENT`, and `ITEM_COUNT`.  
   - **Why**: Enables forecasting of future revenue based on order composition and temporal factors.  
   - **Approach**: Encode categorical variables (one‑hot or target encoding), optionally log‑transform revenue to mitigate skew, evaluate with cross‑validation (RMSE, MAE).
